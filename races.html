How to Read Race Stats

Your race sets your maximum stat caps and grants racial abilities. In-game, you can type help <race name> for more detail.

Defense & Combat

AC = base armor class modifier
RHIT = base hit chance modifier
RMVS = base move/regen modifier

Stat Caps

S = Strength  ·  C = Constitution  ·  D = Dexterity
I = Intelligence  ·  W = Wisdom

Skill & Spell Slots

MSK = Max skill slots  ·  MSP = Max spell slots
Each remort grants +1 MSK and +2 MSP.

Abilities

Racial abilities are always-on traits like infrared, flying, or detection skills.
