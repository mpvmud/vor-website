Remorting

Remorting is a core progression system in Void of Reality. When you reach a required level and have enough killpoints or warpoints, you may choose to remort. This resets your character back to level 1, but grants permanent power increases that carry forward.

Each remort allows you to select five stat increases, limited by your race’s maximum stat caps. In addition, you select one special bonus that enhances combat or survivability.

Remort Requirements

  • Remort 1: Level 55, 500 KPs or 0 WPs
  • Remort 2: Level 55, 1,000 KPs or 0 WPs
  • Remort 3: Level 55, 2,000 KPs or 5 WPs
  • Remort 4: Level 60, 4,000 KPs or 10 WPs
  • Remort 5: Level 60, 8,000 KPs or 15 WPs
  • Remort 6: Level 65, 16,000 KPs or 20 WPs
  • Remort 7: Level 65, 32,000 KPs or 25 WPs
  • Remort 8: Level 65, 64,000 KPs or 30 WPs
  • Remort 9: Level 75, 128,000 KPs or 40 WPs

Stat Choices

  • STR – Increase Strength
  • INT – Increase Intelligence
  • WIS – Increase Wisdom
  • CON – Increase Constitution
  • DEX – Increase Dexterity
  • NONE – Used when capped by racial limits

Special Bonuses

  • hp – +40 hit points
  • mana – +40 maximum mana
  • apb – +3 attack power bonus
  • bs – +3 backstab damage
  • magic – +10 spell damage
  • heal – +10 healing power

Important Notes

  • Remorting resets your level to 1 and removes learned spells and skills.
  • After your first remort, you use the higher HP progression table.
  • After your 1st remort, hunger is disabled.
  • After your 3rd remort, thirst is disabled.
  • Each remort grants +1 skill slot and +2 spell slots.
Example usage: remort str str con dex dex apb transform str str con dex dex apb This grants: +2 Strength +1 Constitution +2 Dexterity +Attack Power bonus
